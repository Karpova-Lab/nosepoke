Build Instructions¶

Assembly¶

Required Tools¶

  • 2mm hex key or screwdriver
  • Soldering iron

Instructions¶

  1. Solder components onto backside PCB

  2. Secure backside PCB to the back of the nosepoke using two M2.5 screws

  3. Solder components onto the perimeter PCBs
    • One board with 82 Ω and white LED
    • Two boards with 82 Ω and IR emitter LED
    • Two boards with solder jumper closed and phototransistor

  4. Place the perimeter PCBs into the channels of the nosepoke. The IR emitters go on the left side, the light goes on top and the phototransistors go on the right side.

  5. Add solder at the joints of the PCBs to create both electrical and mechanical connections.

  6. Using a 2mm hex driver, screw in the push-to-connect fitting.
