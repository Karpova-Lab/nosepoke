Assembly¶

Required Tools¶

  • 2mm hex key or screwdriver

  • Soldering iron

Instructions¶

  1. Secure backside PCB to the back of the nosepoke using two M2.5 screws.

  1. Place the perimeter PCBs into the channels of the nosepoke and solder the joints of the PCBs to create both electrical and mechanical connections.

Note

The IR emitter (D1) goes on the left side, the white LED (D2) goes on top and the phototransistor (Q1) goes on the right side.

  1. Using a 2mm hex key or screwdriver, screw in the push-to-connect fitting.
